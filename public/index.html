Twitter2RSS

Twitter already has RSS feeds. Why would I use this service?

  1. Twitter's RSS feed does not include avatars.
  2. Twitter's RSS feed does not include direct messages.
  3. Twitter's RSS feed commonly truncates the tweets. Blech!
  4. Twitter (and all third-party clients that conform to its API) will not catch @replies if your screen name isn't the first word in the tweet; Twitter2RSS will pick it up no matter where in the message it appears.

OK, you've sold me. How do I use it?

Plug this URL into your feed reader:

http://twitter2rss.anachromystic.com/username/password.rss

Replace username with your Twitter login and password with your Twitter password.

OMG that's so incredibly insecure to put my password in the URL!

Yup, it is. Rest assured I'm not storing your password, but you are at risk of getting it sniffed either on the 'net or over your shoulder. However, Twitter is working on an OAuth service that will eventually allow me to provide a more secure alternative.
