Twitter2RSS

Twitter already has RSS feeds. Why would I use this service?

Twitter's RSS feed does not include direct messages or avatars and commonly truncates the tweets. Blech!

Also, by default, Twitter (and all third-party clients that conform to its API) will not catch any @replies if your screen name isn't the first word in the message; Twitter2RSS will pick it up no matter where in the message it appears.

OK, you've sold me. How do I use it?

Plug this URL into your feed reader:

http://twitter2rss.anachromystic.com/username/password.rss
Replace username with your Twitter login and password with your Twitter password.

OMG that's so incredibly insecure to put my password in the URL!

Yup, it is. Rest assured I'm not storing your password, but you are at risk of getting it sniffed either on the 'net or over your shoulder. I'll be implementing a feature, eventually, which will let you generate a more obfuscated unique URL for security through obscurity. But, for now, this is how it works. Love it or leave it.
